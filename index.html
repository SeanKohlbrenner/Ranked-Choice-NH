THERE IS A PROBLEM WITH NH VOTING.

Politics has grown increasingly polarizing over the past few years in our country. Here in New Hampshire, many voters feel like they have lost their voice or never had one to begin with. This problem affects people of every demographic and crosses party lines. Whether you vote red, blue, or anything in between - we can all agree that we just want to be heard.

WHAT CAN BE DONE?

Awareness is the first step to change! We will guide you through a simple simulation where you will learn about the limitations of our current voting system (first pass the post/FPTP) and we will present a simple alternative that will benefit everyone.

Sandbox:

This simulator will comare RCV and FPTP voting by taking your input and generating voting results. The individual votes will be randomized, so feel free to run the same scenario a couple times and see how the results can differ each time!
